Section 1: Chat Rules

When chating on In-game chat, the map, the site, the forums, or any other chat services that may be offered by BlueLime.Tk there shall be:

  1. No swearing
  2. No spamming
  3. No foul language
  4. No disrespect
  5. No abbreviations that may be interpreted to be swearing
  6. No starred out swear words.
  7. No talking in caps lock
  8. No begging for items or rank/If a staff says stop… you stop!
  9. No asking for Creative or World Edit/We only grant this to a handful, if owner says no… do not ask again!

Section 2: Physical Rules

Across all servers there shall be:

  1. No vandalism/grief of buildings
  2. No excessive trolling
  3. No spam killing
  4. No raiding/stealing
  5. No unauthorized access to admin only/restricted areas of the server
  6. No causing lag
  7. No use of hacked or modified clients
  8. The only additional mods you may have installed on your client while connecting to any of our servers are mods that add HD texture pack support, REI’s minimap, MCCapes, or Optifine.
  9. Fly mod, X-Ray mod, cheat mods, and mods that circumvent intended functionality are strictly prohibited and may result in an instant ban at the server administrations discretion.
  10. No X-Ray texture packs as well

Section 3: MetroBuild Rules

On MetroBuild there shall be:

  1. No machines left unattended/turned on when you’re not on
  2. No asking for reimbursement for items stolen from storage/machine inventory where LWC was not used
  3. No Guns visible in the following locations: LayLands (LayLands police exempt), Haldrie Estates (Haldrie exempt)
  4. Use the forums to: Report Bugs | Report Players | Provide Suggestions and Feedback | Questions and Tutorials

Section 4: Realms of MC Rules

On Realms of MC there shall be:

  1. No tpa/death traps
  2. Use the forums to: Report Bugs | Report Players | Provide Suggestions and Feedback | Questions and Tutorials
  3. Games and Realms should be suggested using the Suggestions and Feedback option above.

Section 5: FanCraft

On FanCraft there shall be:

  1. A visit from the owner of fancraft once per week minimum! No excuses, ifs, ands, or buts!
  2. Use the forums to: Report Bugs | Report Players | Provide Suggestions and Feedback | Questions and Tutorials

Section 6: Network Owner Rules

All network owners shall:

  1. Monitor their servers and fix issues that are reported to them
  2. Not compete with other servers on the network.

Section 7: Staff rules

Staff of all servers shall:

  1. Report any suspicious or malicious activity directly to the owner
  2. Warn players before kicking
  3. Kick players before jailing or muting
  4. Jail players who break physical rules before banning, mute players who break chat rules before banning (if jail or mute is not working or is not an option… a second kick is a good substitute)
  5. Banned players are not permitted back onto the server until the owner approves an unban, they can not be unbanned by the owner until they have submitted a appeal explaining what they did wrong, why it was wrong, and what they will do differently in the future

Section 8: Staff Hiring Rules

Rules for hiring staff:

  1. Before a player can be ranked up to a staff rank they must help to the best of thier ability without any commands.
  2. They must also play regularly on the server
  3. They must earn the respect and trust of the Network Owners/Admins
  4. They must contribute ideas to improve the server and have a house on the server.
